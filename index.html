Booking & shifts

One sauna, many users, zero confusion.

For commercial spas and apartment buildings, the booking engine assigns time slots and heats the sauna to be ready exactly when the next guest arrives — no wasted energy, no cold start.

  • Slot-aware heating. The heater fires up automatically so the room hits target temperature at the booked start time.
  • Resident & staff roles. Set who can book, for how long, and how far ahead.
  • Recurring shifts. Weekly women's, men's and mixed turns handled automatically.
  • Works on any device. A web interface and PWA — no app install required to reserve.

How it works

From legacy heater to connected product in three steps.

STEP 01

Fit the kit

Install our retrofit relay alongside the existing heater. Compatible with standard contactors and Wi-Fi switches — typically under an hour per unit.

STEP 02

Brand the apps

We theme the mobile app, booking site and dashboard with your logo, colours and domain. You approve; we ship to the stores.

STEP 03

Sell the upgrade

Offer remote control and booking as a premium feature to your customers — a new recurring revenue line on hardware you already sell.

Services

We don't just ship the software — we keep it safe and running.

Connecting a heater to the internet raises the bar on safety and upkeep. We carry that responsibility with you across the whole product lifecycle.

SERVICE 01

Safety & regulatory compliance

We help you meet the safety standards that apply to remote-controlled heaters — so connecting your saunas never means cutting corners on certification.

  • Guidance on remote-control safety requirements and standards
  • Built-in safeguards: presence checks, timeouts, max-temp limits
  • Audit trails and documentation for conformity
SERVICE 02

Lifecycle maintenance

The apps and platform are maintained for the full life of your product — updates, security patches, OS and store compliance, and support, handled by us.

  • Ongoing app, firmware-bridge and platform updates
  • Security patching and app-store compliance over time
  • Monitoring, SLAs and technical support for your team
SERVICE 03

Energy tracking & anomaly detection

Every session's energy use is measured and trended. The data drives predictive maintenance and flags anomalies before they become failures.

  • Per-unit consumption and usage tracking
  • Anomaly alerts for unusual draw or heating behaviour
  • Maintenance scheduling driven by real usage data
